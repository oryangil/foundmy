Help Pay for Sweety's Cancer Treatment

Bruna Sedazzari is organizing this campaign.
Protected donation
Since the moment Sweety came into my life three years ago, she has been my constant companion, filling my days with joy and endless love. She’s always been a little ball of energy, racing through the house, cuddling close during thunderstorms, and bringing a sense of calm that only she can provide. But everything changed when I found out she had a tumor 2 months ago. Her once-bright spirit has dimmed as her health deteriorates. She struggles to eat, her energy is gone, and the pain sometimes prevents her from even wagging her tail. Watching her suffer is unbearable, and every day feels like an uphill battle just to keep her comfortable.
Now, the doctor said Sweety is dying and need urgent surgery and to have any chance of reclaiming her life, but the costs are overwhelming. Her medical bills are over $10,000 a cost I can’t shoulder alone. Every donation, every act of kindness, would bring us closer to giving Sweety the fighting chance she deserves. She’s more than just a pet; she’s family, and I'm asking from the bottom of my heart for any support you can give to help her heal and find joy again.
