Help Children in Palestine

Josh Louis is organizing this campaign.
Palestine Protected Donation
Hello, my name is Josh louis. I’m a volunteer here in Palestine, where innocent children are living in unimaginable fear and suffering, caught in a war they never chose. I’ve already given everything I had – every resource, every dollar – but I cannot turn away. I stay here because I cannot bear to see another child live in fear, starve, or cry themselves to sleep because their parents, their home, their sense of safety… everything they once knew has been taken from them. Every day, I hold the hands of children who have lost family members, and I look into eyes that once sparkled with hope, now filled with confusion and despair. Many of them don’t even understand what war is – they only know that it’s destroying their world. I’m just one person, but together, I believe we can make a difference. With your help, we can provide food, clean water, blankets, and shelter to these children. But more than that, we can give them a glimpse of hope in a world that feels like it’s turned its back on them.
Imagine a child who just wants to go back to school, to laugh, to feel safe. Imagine them waking up to find that someone, somewhere, cared enough to make their world a little brighter, a little warmer. Every donation, no matter how small, brings us closer to that dream. Please, join me. Help these children. If you can, donate. And if you cannot donate, then please pray for them. They need all the love, all the faith, and all the hope they can get. These are not just numbers on a news report – these are real lives, real children, with real dreams. Let’s give them a reason to believe in humanity again.
